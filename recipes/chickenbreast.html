Juicy baked chicken breast

The juiciest chicken breast

Grapes, Oranges and pineapples some of the juiciest fruits available and we are about to make a chicken breast with comparable level of juiciness.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • coarse salt
  • pepper
  • any other spices
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Coat both sides of the chicken breast with a thin layer of olive oil.
  3. Sprinkle one side with your corase salt and using a pepper mill grind fresh pepper onto the same side.
  4. Turn over your chicken breast and season the other side.
  5. Place your chicken breast onto a baking sheet or tray and place it into the oven.
  6. As soon as you place your chicken breast into the oven set a timer for 18 minutes.
  7. When 18 minutes have passed immediately pull your chicken breast out of the oven and let it cool for 8 minutes.
  8. Cut open your chicken breast and consume the juiciest chicken breast ever.
