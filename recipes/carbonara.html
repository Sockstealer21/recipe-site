Ingredients

  • 230 grams thick cut bacon
  • 4 ounces Parmesan or Romano cheese or a blend of the two
  • 3 large eggs
  • Freshly Ground black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • retained starchy pasta water
  • 450 grams of spaghettini

Steps

  1. Whisk egg in a bowl and add 90% of your grated cheese
  2. Add freshly ground black pepper into this mixture and whisk
  3. Cut bacon into large chunks
  4. Add bacon into a cold non stick pan and turn the heat on med/high
  5. When the bacon starts to sizzle begin to boil a pot of water and add salt to it
  6. Once the bacon is fully cooked and the fat has rendered out, strain out the grease, keeping only 2 tablespoons of fat in the pan.
  7. Return the pan to medium low heat and add minced garlic. Saute for one minute and then turn off the heat.
  8. When your water starts to boil over add your spaghettini to it and stir occasionally to avoid sticking to the bottom of the pot.
  9. Add the cooked noodles to the pan with the bacon along with â…“ cup of pasta water.
  10. Add the egg and cheese mixture to the pan. Toss riggorously and keep it moving.
  11. Taste for seasoning and assess for thickening. If it needs to be thicker, add more cheese (1 ounce at a time) and mix rigorously.
  12. Serve and enjoy!
