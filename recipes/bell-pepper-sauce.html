Bell pepper sauce

Creamy and sweet pasta sauce

This absolutely amazing bell pepper sauce has an extremely unique flavour compared to typical pasta sauces one might think of and not only does it taste great but its also a very healthy vegeterian sauce. This recipe requires a blender in order to smooth it out and prefferably san marzano tomatoes or tomatoes in their juices if san marzano tomatoes are too difficult to obtain.

Ingredients

  • 2 whole bell peppers any colour
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 3 tbps spicy fermented peppers with the oil they come in
  • Red pepper flake to wanted spice level
  • 500ml heavy cream
  • However much pasta you wish to consume
  • Enough olive oil to coat the bottom of a large skillet
  • 1 can of san marzano tomatoes or 1 can of whole tomatoes with its juices

Steps

  1. Chop the bell peppers, onions and garlic all into thin slices.
  2. Add as much red pepper flake as you want to the garlic slices (its very difficult to over spice this dish).
  3. Heat up your large skillet using med/high heat and add olive oil.
  4. Add your bell peppers into the skillet and cook until they're slightly transparent while not allowing them to stick onto the pan.
  5. Add your onions into your large skillet cook until transluscent.
  6. Add both the garlic slices and the spicy fermented peppers with some of its oil.
  7. After the garlic starts to become translucent Add the entire can of tomatoes and mix everything together and wait for 5 minutes.
  8. Lower your heat to low/med and add your heavy cream and mix everything and simmer for 5 mins.
  9. Load everything into your blender. (I recommend scooping your solids in first then pouring the liquid in.)
  10. Blitz the concoction in your blender whilst regularly allowing heat to escape from the blender.
